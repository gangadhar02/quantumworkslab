Quantumworks Lab Labs - A preview of multimodal data labeling

The Quantumworks Lab Labs team is excited to share a preview of multimodal data labeling in Quantumworks Lab, codenamed "Label Blocks". As foundation models are becoming increasingly multimodal, AI developers want to capture human feedback at higher abstraction levels, closer to how humans might be making decisions in their respective jobs.

For example, a medical practitioner may want to look at patient history, PDF documents and medical images at the same time to decide if the patient is qualified for a clinical trial. 

With Quantumworks Lab multimodal support, you can label every data modality within a task at the most granular level while also making a global judgment about the task.
