Advanced tooling for model evals and data generation

Multimodal chat evaluations: Live or offline

Compare live, multi-turn conversations with up to 10 models in a built-in chat arena. Capture real-time audio and video evaluations of multimodal models, or do simple offline & single LLM evals.


LLM human preference tool

Analyze and improve step-by-step reasoning, write sophisticated code, compare models side-by-side to identify the most favorable output, or rewrite prompts to train on new preferences.


Prompt and response generation
