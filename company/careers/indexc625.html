Work environment

We operate like an early-stage startup, even at our current stage. Our environment is fast-paced and high-intensity, where significant individual ownership and quick decision-making are the norm. We focus relentlessly on impact over process, moving quickly to solve complex problems. 


For those who thrive in this environment, the opportunities are immense. You'll take on expanded responsibilities quickly, with career growth directly tied to your impact. You'll work at the cutting edge of AI development, collaborating closely with industry leaders and shaping the future of artificial intelligence. This is a place where ambitious, high-agency individuals can grow as fast as their impact allows.
