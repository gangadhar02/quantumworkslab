How it works

Accelerate your AI projects with instant access to expert labelers. Build and scale a high-performing data team in four easy steps.

1
Search and filter

Navigate to the Services page in the Quantumworks Lab Platform to explore available AI trainers through the native Quantumworks Lab Alignerr Connect integration. Filter candidates using specific criteria like experience and skillset.

2
Review candidates

Click into Alignerr profiles to view customized interviews and to explore detailed candidate information, including AI-powered insights into their work history, interview transcripts, and labeling experience.

3
Select the best-fit Alignerrs

Select the experts with the right skills by clicking the checkbox in each profile card to identify Alignerrs that match the skills and capabilities you want.

4
Request an Alignerr Connect project

Submit your request by clicking the “Request” button in the platform. A notification will be sent to the Quantumworks Lab team, who will follow up with next steps.
