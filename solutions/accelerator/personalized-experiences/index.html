Categorize and curate product listings faster with foundation models

Training product recommendation models requires lots of data with a wide array of features, all accurately labeled. Labeling 500K+ datarows like this with human annotators is often prohibitively expensive and time consuming. By leveraging foundation models with Quantumworks Lab's seamless data enrichment and curation capabilities, you can scale easily and create a powerful ML solution, fast.


  1. Enrich data with embeddings and natural language via foundation models and domain expertise to cut labeling time in half

  2. Leverage bulk classification in conjunction with these capabilities to curate data for labeling in a few clicks
