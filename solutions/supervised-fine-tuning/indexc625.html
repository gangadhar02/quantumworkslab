Overview

Unlock new AI capabilities with SFT

Supervised fine-tuning (SFT) is a powerful technique that adapts pre-trained models to specific tasks and expands the model’s capabilities. By providing the model with new, carefully curated data, SFT allows you to train the model to perform new actions and align with your specific requirements.

Challenges

The challenges of SFT

SFT comes with its own set of challenges. Acquiring sufficient, high-quality labeled data requires access to vetted, domain-specific experts. Additionally, ensuring consistency and accuracy across annotations is crucial and requires a platform with advanced metrics and real-time reporting.
