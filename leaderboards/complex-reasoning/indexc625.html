Complex reasoning

Last updated: July 10, 2025

The Quantumworks Lab complex reasoning leaderboard rigorously assesses top AI models against some of the most demanding tasks available today. We performed a series of simulations that tested a broad variety of reasoning capabilities, ranging from pure mathematics and programming assessments, to temporal, spatial and more abstract forms of reasoning.
